DiagTestKit - source

A package written by CVB Statistics to estimate the sensitivity and specificity of an experimental diagnostic test kit in accordance with CVB STATWI0002 supporting the 2018 revision to VSM 800.73. 
## Installing v0.5.1 from within R:
######################################

## From source, all platforms, slow.
require(devtools)
install_github("ABS-dev/DiagTestKit", ref = "0.5.1")

## Precompiled binary, Windows + R 3.2.x
install.packages('https://github.com/ABS-dev/DiagTestKit/releases/download/0.5.1/DiagTestKit_0.5.1.zip', repos = NULL)
